About Opereffa

Opereffa is being developed by a team headed by Seref Arikan, working with Professor David Ingram, at University College London, CHIME. Dr Tony Shannon, Chair of the openEHR Clinical Review Board, has been providing clinical feedback from the outset, and the CRB is the main driving body of the project. openEHR mail lists can be used to get in touch with all parties relating to the project.
